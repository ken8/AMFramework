Asset Management Competency Framework

Key Components

The Asset Management Competency Framework for Canadian Communities provides a structured approach to developing and enhancing asset management capabilities within Canadian municipalities. It identifies core competencies across various asset management domains, ensuring a consistent and effective approach to managing public infrastructure.

  • Strategic Alignment: Ensuring asset management activities support organizational goals.
  • Planning and Decision Making: Developing effective asset management plans and making informed decisions.
  • Data and Information Management: Utilizing accurate and reliable data for asset management.
  • Lifecycle Management: Managing assets throughout their entire lifecycle.
  • Financial Management: Ensuring financial sustainability in asset management.
  • Risk Management: Identifying and mitigating risks associated with assets.
  • Stakeholder Engagement: Engaging stakeholders in the asset management process.
